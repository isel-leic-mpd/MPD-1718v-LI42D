#Weather Information Software

## Main requirements
* Get <u>Daily Weather information</u> between two dates
* Daily Weather Information contains
(all temperatures are in celsius):

  * <u>Sunrise time   </u>
  * <u>Sunset time    </u>
  * <u>Moonrise time  </u>
  * <u>Moonset time   </u>
